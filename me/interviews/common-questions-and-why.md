# Interview Common Questions and Why

## Context

This document provides a overview of common interviews questions and meaning behind each one. Interview questions are not about right or wrong, mostly, but interviewers wants to extract the best of our expirience and match with the company while the question is answered.

## Question 1

Can you share more about yourself? Tell more about yourself.

### Why they ask it?
- Matching skills with role. (read the job description)
- To check if you can perform if you are hired. (read culture and company mission)
- To know if you are a match with the teammates and colleagues. (read culture and company mission)

## Question 2

Why you want this job?

### Why they ask it?
- To check if you change between companies you will make this opportunity valueable.
- Avoiding jumper candidates: Job 1 -> Job 2 -> Job 3.


- Check if you are a match between the team and the role.
